# KPI Definitions

This document defines the key performance indicators (KPIs) used within the Sales & Commercial Performance Dashboard.

The purpose of this document is to ensure consistent metric definitions across dashboards, reports, and analytics outputs.

All KPIs are calculated using data from the `mart.fact_sales` table unless otherwise stated.

---

# Revenue

Definition  
Total monetary value of products sold.

Formula  

Revenue = SUM(gross_revenue)

Source Column  

fact_sales.gross_revenue

Business Purpose  

Measures overall sales performance and is the primary indicator of commercial activity.

---

# Profit

Definition  

Total profit generated from product sales.

Formula  

Profit = SUM(profit)

Source Column  

fact_sales.profit

Business Purpose  

Indicates the profitability of sales activity after estimated product cost.

---

# Profit Margin %

Definition  

Percentage of revenue retained as profit.

Formula  

Margin % = Profit / Revenue

Calculation  

SUM(profit) / SUM(gross_revenue)

Business Purpose  

Measures the efficiency and profitability of commercial operations.

---

# Orders

Definition  

Total number of unique orders placed.

Formula  

Orders = COUNT(DISTINCT order_id)

Source Column  

fact_sales.order_id

Business Purpose  

Measures customer purchasing activity.

---

# Units Sold

Definition  

Total quantity of products sold.

Formula  

Units Sold = SUM(quantity)

Source Column  

fact_sales.quantity

Business Purpose  

Measures product sales volume.

---

# Average Order Value (AOV)

Definition  

Average revenue generated per order.

Formula  

AOV = Revenue / Orders

Calculation  

SUM(gross_revenue) / COUNT(DISTINCT order_id)

Business Purpose  

Indicates customer spending behaviour and order size.

---

# Customer Segment Revenue

Definition  

Revenue generated by customer value segment.

Formula  

SUM(gross_revenue) grouped by segment.

Source Tables  

fact_sales  
dim_segment

Business Purpose  

Allows comparison of revenue contribution across:

• Low Value customers  
• Mid Value customers  
• High Value customers

---

# Category Revenue

Definition  

Total revenue generated by product category.

Formula  

SUM(gross_revenue) grouped by category.

Source Tables  

fact_sales  
dim_product

Business Purpose  

Identifies high-performing product categories.

---

# Regional Revenue

Definition  

Total revenue generated by geographic location.

Formula  

SUM(gross_revenue) grouped by city or state.

Source Tables  

fact_sales  
dim_region

Business Purpose  

Supports geographic sales analysis and regional performance comparison.

---

# KPI Governance

These KPI definitions are used consistently across:

• Power BI dashboards  
• Analytical queries  
• Reporting views

Maintaining standard KPI definitions ensures consistent interpretation of performance metrics across stakeholders.
